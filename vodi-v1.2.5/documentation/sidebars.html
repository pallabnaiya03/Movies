Sidebars

Vodi has four sidebars.

Blog Sidebar

Navigate to Vodi > Blog > Blog Layout here choose Right Sidebar or Left Sidebar to enable sidebar in Blog Page.

Output

Settings

  1. Text Widget- Text Widget for blog sidebar.
  2. Search Widget – A search form for your site.
  3. Vodi Posts Widget – Your site’s most recent Posts.
  4. Vodi Tab Widget – Tab Widgets for blog sidebar.
  5. Recent Comments Widget – Widgets For recent comments.

Movie Sidebar

Navigate to Vodi > Movies > Archive Layout here choose Right Sidebar or Left Sidebar to enable sidebar in Movie Page.

Output

Movie Filters

Filter for movies on the site.

Settings

  1. MasVideos Filter movies by Genre Widget – Widgets for A list or dropdown of movies genres.
  2. MasVideos Filter movies by Tags Widget – Widget for Display a list of movie tags to filter movies in your site.
  3. Filter Movies by Year Widget – Display a list of years to filter movies.
  4. MasVideos Filter movies by Rating Widget- Display a list of star ratings to filter movies.

Movie Sidebar

Settings

  1. MasVideos Movies Widgets – Widgets for movies on the site.

Videos Sidebar

Navigate to Vodi > Videos > Archive Layout here choose Right Sidebar or Left Sidebar to enable sidebar in Video Page.

Output

Video Filters

Filter for Videos on the site.

Settings

  1. MasVideos Filter videos by Category Widgets – Display a list of video category to filter videos in your site.
  2. MasVideos Filter videos by Rating Widget – Display a list of star ratings to filter movies.

Video Widgets

Settings

  1. MasVideos Videos widgets – Widgets for Videos on the site.

TV Shows Sidebar

Navigate to Vodi > TV Shows > Archive Layout here choose Right Sidebar or Left Sidebar to enable sidebar in TV Show Page.

Output

TV Shows Filters

Filter for TV Shows on the site.

Settings

  1. MasVideos Search Widget – A search form for your site.
  2. Vodi Filter TV Shows by Letters Widget – Widget for Display a list of letters to filter tv shows.
  3. MasVideos Filter TV Shows by Tags Widget – Display a list of TV Show tags to filter TV shows in your site.
  4. MasVideos Filter TV Shows by Rating Widget – Display a list of star ratings to filter Tv shows.

Settings
