Choosing a Footer Style

Footer v1 is chosen by default header. To change the header style :

  1. Navigate to Vodi.
  2. Click on Footer Tab.You will find Footer Style option with Footer v1, Footer v2, Footer v3, Footer v4, Footer Landingand Footer Coming Soon as five choices presented as dropdown.
  3. Footer landing and Footer coming soon those footers are appeared only corresponding pages.
  4. You find theme style Dark Or Light. You Can Choose anyone.
  5. Choose the dropdown that represents the style of your choice and click on Save Changes.
