Menus & Locations

Introduction

Uneno has 14 menu locations and they are :

  • Primary Menu – Appears on Header v1,v2 and v3
  • Secondary Menu – Appears on Header v3
  • Secondary Nav v3 Menu – Appears on Header v3
  • Offcanvas Menu – Appears on Header v1,v2,v4 and Mobile
  • Navbar Primary – Appears on Header v4
  • Footer v1 Quick Links – Appears on Footer v1
  • Footer v2 Primary Menu – Appears on Footer v2
  • Footer v2 Secondary Menu – Appears on Footer v2
  • Footer v2 Tertiary Menu – Appears on Footer v2
  • Footer v4 Menu – Appears on Footer v4
  • Landing Menu – Appears on Landing Header v1
  • Comingsoon Offcanvas Menu – Appears on Comingsoon page
  • Landing footer Menu – Appears on Landing footer
  • Social Media – Appears on Footer v1,v2,landing and comingsoon

All the menus can be assigned from Appearance > Menus > Manage Location
